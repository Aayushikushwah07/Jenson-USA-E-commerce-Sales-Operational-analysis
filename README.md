🚴 Jenson USA Sales Analysis

This project analyzes sales data of Jenson USA to extract meaningful business insights using SQL.
The goal of this analysis is to answer advanced business questions related to product performance, customer behavior, staff efficiency, and store operations.

📌 Project Objectives

1.The following business problems were solved in this analysis:

2.Find the total number of products sold by each store along with the store name.

3.Calculate the cumulative sum of quantities sold for each product over time.

4.Identify the product with the highest total sales (Quantity × Price) for each category.

5.Find the customer who spent the most money on orders.

6.Determine the highest-priced product in each category.

7.Calculate the total number of orders placed by each customer per store.

8.List staff members who have not made any sales.

9.Identify the top 3 most sold products based on quantity.

10.Calculate the median value of the price list.

11.List all products that have never been ordered (using EXISTS).

12.Identify staff members who made more sales than the average sales of all staff members.

13.Find customers who have ordered products from every category.

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
-SQL (MySQL / PostgreSQL compatible queries)
-Window Functions
-Subqueries
-Aggregate Functions
-EXISTS clause
-GROUP BY & HAVING

📊 Key Insights
-Identified top-performing stores based on product volume.
-Determined the most profitable products within each category.
-Discovered high-value customers contributing maximum revenue.
-Evaluated staff performance and identified inactive staff members.
-Analyzed product demand trends using cumulative sales.
-Found unsold products to improve inventory management.

🎯 Business Impact
This analysis helps:
Improve inventory planning
Optimize staff performance
Identify high-revenue customers
Support data-driven decision making
Improve overall store profitability
